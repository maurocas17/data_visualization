Flight Performance
A flight's on-time performance can be affected by delays, fight cancellations and diversions. Delays can be categorized into:  Air Carrier. The cause was within the airline's control (e.g. maintenance or crew problems, aircraft cleaning, baggage loading, fueling, etc.).   Extreme Weather such as tornado, blizzard or hurricane.    National Aviation System (NAS) which refers to a broad set of conditions, such as non-extreme weather conditions, airport operations, heavy traffic volume, and air traffic control.   Late-arriving aircraft where a previous flight with same aircraft arrived late, causing the present flight to depart late.   Security caused by evacuation of a terminal or concourse, re-boarding of aircraft because of security breach, inoperative screening equipment and/or long lines in excess of 29 minutes at screening areas. For more details, see RITA -Understanding flight delays and cancellations. The chart below compares flight performance among Airline Carriers and Airports. Majority of delays is caused by Late-Arriving Aircraft, followed mostly by NAS, then Air Carrier. The portion of this delay is shown for each of the Airlines and Airports on the right-side. Highlighted on the right-side is the Airline or Airport with the highest percentage of performance hits. We can see here that even-though Late-Arriving Aircraft is the number one cause of delay, it is not the number one cause of delay for that Airline or Airport. Flight performance is not only measured by the total delays, cancellations and diversions, but also by the volume of on-time flights for that Airline or Airport.

Flight data includes the top 4 Largest US Airlines and the top 12 busiest Airports in the United States from January 2014 to August 2015. Count is in thousands unit. Total flight is the sum of on-time flight and delays (i.e. aircraft, nas, weather, late, security), cancellations and diversions. % of Total Flights is the percentage of that delay/cancellation/diversion against the total flight for that carrier, airport or overall total flight for that year and month.
